\section{Remaining Works}

The following key components are planned for future implementation to enhance the site analysis platform for opening a cafe:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Heat Map Visualization:} \\
    Develop and integrate a heat map feature to visually represent popular or viable areas for opening a cafe. This will help users quickly identify high-potential locations based on aggregated POI data and other relevant metrics.

    \item \textbf{Demographic and Neighborhood Analysis:} \\
    Incorporate demographic data and neighborhood characteristics into the analysis. This includes integrating population density, age distribution, income levels, and other socio-economic factors to provide a more comprehensive evaluation of each location.

    \item \textbf{Area Comparison and Ranking:} \\
    Implement tools to compare multiple areas side-by-side, allowing users to rank locations based on various criteria such as POI density, demographic suitability, and accessibility.

    \item \textbf{User Analysis History and Comparison:} \\
    Enable saving of users' past analysis sessions, allowing them to revisit previous results and perform direct comparisons between different locations or time periods.

    \item \textbf{Enhanced Reporting and Export Options:} \\
    Add functionality for generating detailed reports of analysis results, including charts and maps, with options to export these reports in PDF or other formats for offline review and sharing.

    \item \textbf{User Feedback and Customization:} \\
    Implement features for users to provide feedback on analysis results and customize the weighting of different factors according to their business strategy or preferences.
\end{enumerate}

\noindent
These enhancements will provide users with deeper insights and greater flexibility, supporting more informed and strategic decisions for cafe site selection.
